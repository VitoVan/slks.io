Yet another URL shortener


http://www.example.com/here-is-a-very-long-url-which-you-wanna-make-it-shorter

Prefix it with `slks.io/`

slks.io/http://www.example.com/here-is-a-very-long-url-which-you-wanna-make-it-shorter

Then you got: slks.io/e
